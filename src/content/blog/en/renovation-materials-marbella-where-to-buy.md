## Frequently asked questions

### Do I need a permit to buy renovation materials?
No, you do not need a permit to purchase renovation materials. However, you might need a building permit (licencia de obra) from your local town hall for the renovation work itself, depending on its scope. Always check local regulations before starting any significant project.

### Can I get materials delivered to my home in Marbella?
Yes, most renovation material suppliers on the Costa del Sol offer delivery services to Marbella. Delivery costs and times can vary based on the supplier, order size, and your exact location. It is always best to confirm delivery details when placing your order.

### Are Spanish material sizes different from other countries?
Spain primarily uses the metric system for measurements, which is standard across Europe. This means dimensions for tiles, wood, and other materials will be in centimetres and metres. If you are used to imperial measurements, be sure to convert carefully during planning.
