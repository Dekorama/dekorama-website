## What Does a Full Home Renovation Entail?
A 'reforma integral' or full home renovation goes beyond cosmetic updates. It involves a comprehensive overhaul of your property, often including structural changes, reconfiguring layouts, and updating all major systems. This approach ensures a cohesive design and addresses any underlying issues, providing a fresh start for your home.

### Key Areas Covered
A complete renovation typically includes:
*   **Demolition and structural work**: Removing walls, creating new openings, or reinforcing existing structures.
*   **Electrical and plumbing systems**: Updating or replacing old wiring, pipes, and drainage for safety and efficiency.
*   **Flooring**: Installing new [porcelain tiles in Málaga](/en/porcelanicos-malaga), wood, or other flooring materials throughout the property.
*   **Walls and ceilings**: Plastering, painting, and finishing to achieve a modern aesthetic.
*   **Kitchens**: Designing and installing [custom kitchens](/en/cocinas-a-medida) with new cabinetry, countertops, and appliances.
*   **Bathrooms**: Completely transforming spaces with new fixtures, tiling, and layouts for [complete bathroom renovations](/en/banos-completos).
*   **Windows and doors**: Replacing old units for improved insulation, security, and aesthetics.
*   **Outdoor spaces**: Landscaping, terracing, and pool area renovations for properties with exterior areas.

## How Much Do Full Home Renovations Cost on the Costa del Sol?
The cost of [full home renovations on the Costa del Sol](/en/reformas-integrales) varies significantly based on several factors. As a general guide, you can expect to pay anywhere from €400 to €1,200 per square metre for a comprehensive renovation. This range accounts for different levels of finish and complexity.

### Cost Factors to Consider
*   **Scope of work**: Minor updates versus structural changes, reconfigurations, and system replacements.
*   **Material quality**: Choosing between standard, mid-range, or [premium materials](/en/materiales-premium) for tiles, flooring, fixtures, and fittings significantly impacts the budget.
*   **Property size and age**: Larger properties naturally incur higher costs, and older homes may require more extensive foundational or system overhauls.
*   **Design complexity**: Custom designs, bespoke joinery, or intricate architectural features will increase costs.
*   **Permits and fees**: Local council permits and technical architect fees are mandatory expenses.
*   **Accessibility**: Properties with difficult access can incur higher labour and logistics costs.

### Budgeting for Your Project
It is always wise to allocate an additional 10-15% of your total budget for unforeseen circumstances. ## How Long Does a Full Renovation Take?
The timeline for a full home renovation on the Costa del Sol can vary greatly depending on the project's scale and complexity. While smaller renovations might take a couple of months, a complete overhaul of a typical apartment or villa usually requires 3 to 8 months from the initial planning stages to final completion.

### Typical Project Phases
*   **Planning and design (4-8 weeks)**: This includes initial consultations, architectural plans, material selection, and permit applications.
*   **Demolition and structural work (2-4 weeks)**: Removing existing elements and making any necessary structural changes.
*   **System installations (4-8 weeks)**: Updating electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems.
*   **Finishing work (8-16 weeks)**: Installing flooring, tiling, plastering, painting, fitting kitchens and bathrooms, and final touches.
*   **Inspections and handover**: Final checks and official completion.

### Managing Expectations
Delays can occur due to permit approvals, material availability, or unforeseen structural issues. A professional renovation company will keep you informed of progress and any potential adjustments to the timeline. Clear communication and realistic expectations are key to a successful project.

## Frequently asked questions

### Do I need a permit for a full home renovation in Benalmádena?
Yes, a full home renovation in Benalmádena typically requires a building permit (Licencia de Obras Mayores) from the local town hall. This ensures your project complies with urban planning regulations and safety standards. A professional renovation company can assist with the application process.

### Can I live in my property during a full renovation?
Living in your property during a full renovation is generally not recommended, especially for extensive projects involving structural changes or utility shutdowns. It can be disruptive, noisy, and potentially unsafe. Most clients arrange alternative accommodation for the duration of the work.
