## How Much Does a Full Renovation Cost for a Holiday Apartment in Marbella?

Understanding the financial investment is crucial for assessing profitability. Renovation costs in Marbella can vary widely based on several factors.

### Factors Influencing Renovation Costs

*   **Apartment Size**: Larger apartments naturally require more materials and labour.
*   **Scope of Work**: A complete overhaul, including structural changes and full system replacements, will cost more than a purely cosmetic update.
*   **Material Quality**: Choosing standard, mid-range, or luxury finishes significantly impacts the budget. [Premium materials](/en/materiales-premium) offer durability and a high-end look, justifying a higher rental price.
*   **Labour Costs**: These vary between contractors and depend on the complexity and specialisation required for the work.
*   **Permits and Fees**: Administrative costs for obtaining necessary building permits must be factored in.

### Cost Benchmarks for Marbella

As a general guide for [renovations in Marbella](/en/reformas-marbella), you can expect the following per square metre:

*   **Basic Renovation (Cosmetic)**: €400 - €550 per square metre. This includes painting, minor flooring updates, and basic kitchen/bathroom refreshes without major structural or system changes.
*   **Mid-Range Renovation (Standard)**: €550 - €750 per square metre. This typically covers new kitchens and bathrooms, full flooring replacement, updated electrical and plumbing, and some layout adjustments.
*   **High-End/Luxury Renovation (Comprehensive)**: €750 - €1,000+ per square metre. This involves a complete overhaul, including structural modifications, premium finishes, custom joinery, and high-specification installations.

For example, a 70m² apartment could range from €28,000 for a basic renovation to over €70,000 for a luxury transformation. Always obtain detailed, itemised quotes from reputable companies like DEKORAMA to get an accurate estimate for your specific project.
