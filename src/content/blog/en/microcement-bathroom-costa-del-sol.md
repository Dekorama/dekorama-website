## What is Microcement and Why is it Ideal for Bathrooms?
Microcement is a polymer-modified cement-based coating, applied in thin layers to create a smooth, continuous surface. It is composed of cement, water-based resins, additives, and mineral pigments. This combination results in a highly durable, flexible, and adhesive material that can be applied over existing surfaces like tiles, plasterboard, or concrete, eliminating the need for extensive demolition.

### Seamless Aesthetics and Modern Appeal
One of microcement's most significant draws is its seamless finish. Without grout lines, it creates an expansive, uninterrupted look that makes bathrooms feel larger and more open. This minimalist aesthetic aligns perfectly with contemporary design trends, offering a sophisticated backdrop for any style of decor.

### Waterproof and Hygienic Properties
When properly sealed, microcement is completely waterproof, making it an excellent choice for wet areas such as shower walls, floors, and even sinks. This impermeability prevents water penetration and protects against humidity, a common concern in coastal regions like the Costa del Sol. Its non-porous surface also inhibits the growth of bacteria and fungi, ensuring a highly hygienic environment.

### Durability and Low Maintenance
Despite its thin application, microcement is remarkably hard-wearing and resistant to daily wear and tear. It can withstand heavy foot traffic, impacts, and abrasions, making it a long-lasting solution for busy bathrooms. Maintenance is straightforward, requiring only regular cleaning with neutral detergents.

### Versatility in Design
Microcement offers immense design flexibility. It comes in a wide array of colours, from natural earthy tones to vibrant hues, and can be finished with various textures, from smooth and polished to more rustic. This versatility allows for customisation to match any interior design vision, creating a cohesive look throughout your bathroom.

## The Microcement Installation Process: What to Expect
Understanding the steps involved in applying microcement can help you appreciate the craftsmanship and planning required for a successful bathroom renovation.

### 1. Surface Preparation
The existing surface must be clean, dry, stable, and perfectly level. Any imperfections are repaired, and a primer is applied to ensure optimal adhesion of the microcement.

### 2. Application of Base Layers
Once the primer is dry, several thin layers of base microcement are carefully trowelled. This stage builds up the strength and foundation of the microcement system.

### 3. Application of Finish Layers
After the base layers cure, the decorative finish layers of microcement are applied. This is where the desired colour and final texture are achieved by skilled applicators.

### 4. Sealing and Protection
Once the finish layers are dry, multiple coats of a high-quality sealer are applied. This sealer is vital for making the microcement waterproof, stain-resistant, and highly durable.

### 5. Curing Time
While the surface will be touch-dry relatively quickly, microcement requires a curing period to reach its full hardness. Light use is typically possible after 24-48 hours, but it is advisable to avoid heavy use for at least 7 days. Full curing can take up to 28 days.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is microcement slippery in a bathroom?
When properly applied and sealed, microcement can be made slip-resistant. Applicators can incorporate anti-slip additives into the final sealer coats or choose specific textures to enhance grip, making it safe for bathroom floors and shower trays.

### Can microcement be applied over existing tiles?
Yes, one of the main advantages of microcement is its ability to be applied directly over most existing surfaces, including tiles. This eliminates the need for demolition, saving time, reducing mess, and lowering overall renovation costs.

### How long does a microcement bathroom renovation take?
A typical microcement bathroom renovation usually takes between 3 to 7 days, depending on the size of the bathroom and the complexity of the project. This includes surface preparation, multiple layers of microcement, and sealing.

### Is microcement suitable for shower walls and floors?
Absolutely. Microcement is highly waterproof when properly sealed, making it an excellent choice for shower walls and floors. Its seamless nature prevents water penetration and mould growth, ensuring a hygienic and durable wet area.

### What maintenance does a microcement bathroom require?
Microcement bathrooms are low maintenance. Regular cleaning with neutral pH detergents is sufficient. Avoid abrasive cleaners or harsh chemicals, which can damage the sealer.
