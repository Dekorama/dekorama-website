## What Does a Full Renovation (Reforma Integral) Entail?

A "reforma integral" or full renovation goes beyond cosmetic updates. It involves a comprehensive transformation of your property, often including structural changes, new installations, and complete redesigns. This type of project requires meticulous planning and coordination across multiple trades.

### Scope of Work

A full renovation typically covers every aspect of your home. This can include reconfiguring floor plans, updating electrical and plumbing systems, installing new flooring and tiling, replacing windows and doors, and completely redesigning spaces like kitchens and bathrooms. It is a holistic approach aimed at modernising and optimising your living environment.

### Benefits of a Coordinated Approach

Opting for a company that manages the entire project from start to finish ensures consistency in design and execution. A single point of contact simplifies communication, reduces stress, and minimises potential delays or misunderstandings that can arise when coordinating multiple independent contractors. It also ensures all elements work together harmoniously, from structural integrity to the final aesthetic touches.

### Licensing and Insurance

Always verify that the company holds all necessary licenses and comprehensive insurance. This protects you from liability in case of accidents or damage during the renovation. Reputable companies will readily provide this documentation.

### Communication and Transparency

Effective communication is paramount. Choose a company that is responsive, clear, and proactive in keeping you informed. They should be transparent about the process, potential challenges, and any changes. For international clients, clear communication in English is often a significant advantage.

### Detailed Quotes and Contracts

Insist on a detailed, itemised quote that clearly outlines all costs, materials, and labour. A comprehensive contract should specify the scope of work, payment schedule, timeline, and dispute resolution mechanisms. Avoid companies offering vague or overly simplistic estimates.

### References and Reviews

Ask for references from previous clients and check online reviews. Speaking directly with past customers can provide valuable insights into the company's reliability, professionalism, and ability to deliver on promises. This is often the best indicator of a company's true performance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need permits for a full renovation in Marbella?
Yes, a full renovation in Marbella almost always requires permits from the local Town Hall. The specific type of permit depends on the scope of work, especially if it involves structural changes. A professional renovation company will manage this process for you.

### How long does a full renovation typically take?
The duration of a full renovation varies greatly. For a standard apartment or villa, it can range from 3 to 6 months. Larger or more complex projects, especially those involving significant structural work, may take 9 months or longer. Your chosen company should provide a clear timeline.

### What is the difference between a contractor and a full renovation company?
A contractor typically handles specific parts of a project, like plumbing or tiling. A full renovation company, like DEKORAMA, offers a comprehensive, integrated service, managing all aspects from design and permits to construction and final finishes, providing a single point of contact.

### Can I renovate my property if I am not in Spain?
